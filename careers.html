Publish date: 2024-08-12

Here’s “the really, really” of learning a language: there are no shortcuts. Making progress in a language is like learning how to play the piano or training to finish a marathon: you need to make it a regular weekly habit to take classes with certified teachers and practice. According to the US State Dept and Council of Europe for foreign languages, it takes 100 class hours to advance a level and 600 hours to become fluent. Our courses are centered around human interaction and maximum conversation time. Certified teachers lead live group classes with a maximum of 5 students.

The curriculum is highly structured, based on CEFR European methodology and takes you from just getting started (A1) to fluency (C1), combining grammar, vocabulary, and real-life language and cultural context. To expose you to a range of accents and speaking styles, we rotate teachers and students from more than 120 countries. And best of all, it’s possible from your home laptop or mobile phone, anywhere in the world. 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Flexible for your forever-changing busy schedule.

Our promise to you. If you build a regular habit of learning, you will get much better, even become fluent. Anybody can do it. We’ll be with you every step of the way.

One of the best places to work in Berlin!
