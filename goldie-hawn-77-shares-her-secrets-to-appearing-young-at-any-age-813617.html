Goldie Hawn, 77, Shares Her Secrets to Appearing Young at Any Age

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Whenever we hear that Goldie Hawn is getting closer to her 80s, we can’t help but be amazed. Her commitment to yoga has helped her maintain a fit body and a calm mind, which seems to keep her youthful. But it’s not just exercise that keeps her looking radiant — Goldie has discovered a worry-free skincare routine that gives her a glowing complexion, something we all could learn from.

She doesn’t worry about her imperfections.

Goldie believes that your mindset strongly influences beauty. It’s easy to feel less confident by comparing yourself to others and obsessing over your flaws. However, Goldie has a simple approach to this issue: “I also glance a lot at my face, but I don’t study it. I’m not a studier of my face, of my beauty.”

Her skincare routine is simple and straightforward.

AF Archive / Mary Evans Picture Library / East News, Allstar / Graham Whitby Boot / Mary Evans Picture Library / East News, Acosta David / Image Press Agency / ABACA / Abaca / East News

Goldie keeps her skincare routine simple and fuss-free. She doesn’t believe in spending hours preparing her skin before bed. Instead, she sticks to washing her face every night, massaging it for about 3 minutes, and applying her favorite creams: “And then I wake up in the morning and do the same thing!”

She also shared a few of her tricks, and they’re pretty minimal: “I don’t do much. I was using coconut oil for a while on my face. My mom used to do lemon — she’d squeeze lemon and put it on her face.” Best of all, these tricks are affordable and won’t break the bank.

Anyone can use her tips.

Album Online / EAST NEWS, Jordan Strauss / Invision / AP / East News

Goldie Hawn reminds us that creams and masks aren’t the only important aspects of skincare. She emphasizes the need to protect skin from the sun: “I put on a little sun guard in the morning when I remember, and the rest of it is just making sure that you’re hydrated and that you eat a lot of oil-based foods or oils and smile a lot.”

She has passed on the wisdom to her daughter that true beauty stems from within.

Adriana M. Barraza / WENN.com / agefotostock / East News

According to Kate, she and her mother prioritize their nutrition by incorporating healthy habits into their diet. They enjoy drinking green juice and taking teaspoons of olive oil. While they appreciate beauty products, Kate emphasizes that the real beauty lessons she learned from her mom center around a healthy and active lifestyle, including consuming nutritious and green foods.

Hudson expressed gratitude for everything her mother brought into her life. She believes it’s no coincidence that her mom possesses an extraordinary zest for life and a joyful spirit. Hudson recognizes her mother’s profound depths, describing how she has faced challenges head-on, paved the way for others, and persevered through difficult times without accepting defeat.

She likes to keep everything as natural as she can.

Goldie keeps her routine all-natural, and that includes her hair. She shared, “I know it sounds strange, but I’ve never colored my hair. I just put a regular rinse on it because I don’t have any chemicals on it.” Maybe her name has something to do with her beautiful golden locks too.

Goldie Hawn is a wonderful role model for aging gracefully, and we can gain valuable insights from her and other famous women who openly share their fitness and skincare tips with us.
