How 15 Popular Child Stars Look Today and Who They Ended Up Marrying

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Many of us grew up admiring the cute and talented child stars who captured our hearts on the big and small screens. From the iconic Lindsay Lohan to Daryl Sabara, aka the Spy Kids boy, we watched them grow up right before our eyes. In this article, we reveal the unknown and fascinating details of 15 popular child stars, including their surprising transformations and marital status.

1. Raven-Symoné

Raven-Symoné, best known for her lead role in the Disney Channel’s hit show That’s So Raven, has tied the knot with Miranda Maday. The couple had a private ceremony in June 2020, surrounded by their loved ones. Raven-Symoné has been vocal about her support for the LGBTQ+ community.

2. Christina Ricci

EAST NEWS, Invision/Invision/East News

Known for her role in The Addams Family (1991), Christina Ricci made her debut in 1990 when she was 9. Currently, the actress has continued starring in movies and on television. Christina Ricci got married for the second time in 2021, her husband is hairstylist Mark Hampton.

3. Lindsay Lohan

The star of movies like The Parent Trap (1998) and Freaky Friday (2003) started her career as a model when she was just three years old. Now Lindsay Lohan is starring in films too, with Falling for Christmas (2022) coming later this year. She and financier Bader Shammas recently got married, which she announced on July 2nd, saying she’s “the luckiest woman in the world.”

4. Joe Jonas

EVERETT COLLECTION/EAST NEWS, Invision/Invision/East News

One of the Jonas Brothers, Joe, was 17 when the pop band released their first album titled It’s About Time. In 2019, he and Sophie Turner (a Game of Thrones star who also started her acting career quite early) tied the knot. The couple is expecting their second child at the moment.

5. Ashley Tisdale

This Disney star succeeded thanks to her roles in The Suite Life of Zack & Cody (2005–2008) and High School Musical (2006), but she appeared in many advertisements and minor TV and theater roles even before that. Now she has her own business ventures and appears on TV and in the movies. She has been married to Christopher French since 2014, and their daughter Jupiter Iris French was born in 2021.

6. Alexa PenaVega

Most of us remember Alexa PenaVega for her role in the Spy Kids movies, but she started her career even earlier: her first role was in 1996 in Twister. Nowadays, Alexa is known as an actress and singer. She’s married to former Big Time Rush actor and singer Carlos PenaVega. The couple has three kids together.

7. Bridgit Mendler

Another Disney Channel star, Bridgit Mendler, started her acting career in 2004 when she was 12. Since then, she’s appeared in Alvin and the Chipmunks: The Squeakquel (2009), Wizards of Waverly Place, Good Luck Charlie (2010-2014), etc. In 2019, the actress announced that she married Griffin Cleverly.

8. Matthew Lewis

English actor Matthew Lewis quickly became famous after he played Neville Longbottom in the Harry Potter series. However, this wasn’t his first movie: his debut happened in Some Kind of Life (1996). At present, Matthew Lewis continues his career with more TV appearances. In 2018, he married Angela Jones, an event manager.

9. David Henrie

The star of Wizards of Waverly Place (2007-2012) first got a regular role on the sitcom The Pitts (2003) when he was 13. Now he also directs movies and writes scripts. He got married in 2017 to former Miss Delaware, Maria Cahill. The couple is expecting their third child now.

10. Corbin Bleu

Most famous for his role in High School Musical, Corbin Bleu has not only played a singing basketball player. His debut was a role in the adventure comedy film Catch That Kid in 2004, where his co-star was Kristen Stewart. He’s still acting to this day, and he’s married to actress Sasha Clements.

11. Debby Ryan

Debby Ryan, now an actress and a singer, started in professional theaters when she was 7. Then she was discovered by Disney Channel. She tied the knot with musician Josh Dun in 2019.

12. Josh Peck

Drake & Josh star, Josh Peck, is happily married to his longtime love, Paige O’Brien. The couple tied the knot in June 2017. Peck, who rose to fame for his role in the hit Nickelodeon show, has since become a prominent actor, comedian, and social media personality.

13. Britney Spears

Britney Spears first demonstrated her vocal talent in a Star Search competition in 1992 when she was 10. Her song “...Baby One More Time” became a hit in 1998 when the pop star was 17. Over the years, her career had ups and downs, but lately, she regained control over her life and married actor and fitness trainer Sam Asghari in June 2022.

14. Nathan Kress

The actor, who many people recognize from his role in iCarly (2007–2012), a sitcom revived in 2021, started his career when he was just four years old: he appeared in commercials and some films. Later, he added directing and voice-over experience to his portfolio. Nathan married actress and stunt performer London Elise Moore in 2015. The couple has two daughters.

15. Daryl Sabara

Daryl Sabara, the Spy Kids boy, is all grown up and hitched to pop superstar Meghan Trainor. The couple decided to tie the knot in 2019 and welcomed their first child, a bouncing baby boy named Riley, in 2021.
