Bingham University admission requirements and application

Below are the general entry requirements you must meet to gain admission into the institution.

UTME candidates

  • All candidates must have taken the 2023/2024 UTME Examination and obtained a minimum score of 150.
  • The minimum qualification for Degree programmes is five (5) O/Level Credits in relevant subjects, including Mathematics and English, at up to two (2) sittings.

Bingham University application process

Below is a step-by-step guide on how to apply for admission at Bingham University.

  • Visit the official Bingham University portal and create an account if you don't have one.
  • Once an account has been created, an email containing an activation link will be sent to the specified email address.
  • After account activation, login to the portal and use the 'My Payments' menu to access the application form payment page. On the payment page, a transaction can be initiated using the 'Pay with Remita' button, after which a payment slip for N2000 will be generated.
  • Take the payment slip to any bank branch to complete the transaction, or the Bingham University school fees payment can be made online using ATM cards.

    How to submit the completed forms

    After completing the payment transaction, the online application forms will be accessible. Below are the simple steps for submitting them.

  • Click the 'Form submission' button.
  • Print a hard copy of the submitted forms.
  • Submit the hard copy to the Academic Office on or before the screening day.

    • The students should obtain the following documents online and submit them with their admission forms.

    • Character assessment form
    • Applicant's confidential report
    • Guidance and counselling form
    • UTME/DE screening assessment form
