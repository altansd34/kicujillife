The Open University | Scotland.org

Publish date: 2024-08-08

The Open University believes world-class university education should be open to anyone. So, wherever you are in the world, you can open up your future with the OU.

Since 1969 they’ve been teaching across time zones, giving students in over 180 countries a flexible alternative to traditional, university education – with all the support and teaching excellence you’d expect from the world’s leading distance learning university.

The OU offers over 200 globally recognised qualifications across a wide range of subjects, from undergraduate language courses to MBAs and PhDs including undergraduate degrees, postgraduate degrees, certificates, and diplomas, many accredited by leading professional bodies. The OU has more than 20,000 students based in Scotland. Studying with the OU allows you to improve or transform your career and earn while you learn, wherever you are based.
