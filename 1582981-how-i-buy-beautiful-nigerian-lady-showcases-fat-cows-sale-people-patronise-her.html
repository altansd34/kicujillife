&quot;How Can I Buy?&quot; Beautiful Nigerian Lady Showcases Fat Cows For Sale, People Want to Patronise Her

Publish date: 2024-08-13
  • A Nigerian lady was spotted in a video showing off fat cows, sampling them for sale to potential customers
  • In the video, the lady mentioned that one cow cost N500,000 at the time she made the post on TikTok
  • She stated that she sells male cows, and some of her followers said they would like to patronise her

A Nigerian lady who deals in livestock has shared a video on TikTok to show off some fat cows online.

In the video, the lady, @maureenhd1, pointed at the cows and mentioned their individual prices.

According to Maureen, one cow at the time she posted the video cost between N450,000 to N570,000.

She, however, added that there was a delivery fee of N50,000 if the buyer wanted the cow brought to them.

The video caught many people's attention; some were surprised that a lady was selling cows.

When contacted, Maureen told Legit.ng that the cost of cows is influenced by the cost of feeding them.

Her words:

"The price of cow has fluctuated slightly over the past few months, due to changes in the market and inflation wise but overall it's remained relatively stable for now. The cost of feed have been the main factor affecting the price of cow recently."

Maureen who is from Anambra state told Legit.ng that she has been in the cow business for three years, having started in 2021. She said:

"I've been in the cow business since 2021 after my youth service. It was a natural choice for me to go into the cow business."

She noted that she resides in Kano state and that she made the decision to go into the cow business after her NYSC.

She said:

"The decision to sell cows was not only driven by my passion for animals, but also by my desire to do something that would challenge me and help me grow. The cow business has allowed me to test my limits, hone my skills, and learn new things every day. It's a rewarding and constantly evolving experience that I wouldn't trade for anything. And the satisfaction of providing a quality service to my customers is the icing on the cake!"
