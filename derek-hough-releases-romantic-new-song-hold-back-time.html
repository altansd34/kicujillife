Dancing with the Stars judge Derek Hough has released a new original song called “Hold Back Time.” The song’s cover image features a shot of Hough and his fiancée Hayley Erbert. The track features romantic lyrics.

Derek Hough Releases New Song ‘Hold Back Time’

The song’s cover art shows Hough and Erbert embracing each other under water. Hough is wearing a white shirt, while Erbert is in a fringed dress. The couple gazes into each other’s eyes as Hough reaches up to touch Erbert’s face.

“This heart is your home, hope you know this / You won’t be alone, let me show it,” Hough sings to open the piano-backed track. “Can we take it slow, and just notice / There’s so little time, can’t control it.”

In the song’s chorus, Hough sings that he’s “caught in forever with you” and doesn’t “want this moment to end.” He wants them to “hold back time” so they can stay that way for longer.

Hough is credited as a writer on the track, alongside Dustin Brian Burnett as the composer. It was produced by Burnett and Zayde Wølf.

When Are Hough, Erbert Getting Married?

Hough and Erbert got engaged last June, and fans have been wondering when they plan to finally tie the knot. In May of this year, Hough confirmed that they’re planning to get married this year. He’s also said that fellow pro dancer Mark Ballas will be his best man.

The second half of the year is shaping up to be very busy for the couple, as they’re about to embark on Hough’s “Symphony of Dance” tour around the country. The first performance is in late September, with dates lined up into December.

In the meantime, the couple has been doing plenty of performing together. Last month, they danced together in the music video for “Turning Page” by Sleeping at Last.
