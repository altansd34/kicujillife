America’s Got Talent: The Champions star Angelina Jordan has released a new original song called “Love Don’t Let Me Go.” The 17-year-old singer also shared a stylish visualizer to go along with the jazzy new track.

AGT: The Champions Star Angelina Jordan Releases New Song

Angelina channels iconic artists like Adele and Amy Winehouse on her new song, which shows off the powerful, husky voice that won her Norway’s Got Talent in 2014 and took her to the Finals of AGT: The Champions in 2020.

“Love, I really wish you had a face / Maybe I could beg for you to stay,” Angelina sings on the track’s chorus. “I’m on the floor, I’m begging, oh / Love, don’t let me go / If only I could look you in the eye / There wouldn’t be no place for you to hide / I’m on the floor, I’m begging, oh / Love, don’t let me go.”

The song was released on Friday with a black and white visualizer in which Angelina sports a ’60s-style hairdo and drives around in a vintage car. The shots are intercut with her performing the song at a microphone.

Angelina Reached The Champions Finals

After winning Norway’s Got Talent at age 8, Angelina competed on the second season of America’s Got Talent: The Champions. For her audition, she shared her take on “Bohemian Rhapsody” by Queen, earning the Golden Buzzer from judge Heidi Klum.

This sent Angelina straight to the Finals, where she performed Elton John’s “Goodbye Yellow Brick Road.” During the season finale, she also delivered a performance of “Someone You Loved” by Lewis Capaldi alongside fellow finalists Tyler Butler-Figueroa and Kseniya Simonova.

Unfortunately, Angelina didn’t earn enough votes from the Superfans to make it into the Top 5. However, she’s continued to perform in the years since her appearance on AGT. In 2021, she released another original single called “7th Heaven.”
