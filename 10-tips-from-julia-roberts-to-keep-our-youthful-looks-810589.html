10 Tips From Julia Roberts to Keep Our Youthful Looks

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Throughout her entire career, which began in 1987 on TV, Julia Roberts has been named the most beautiful woman in the world 5 times in People magazine’s annual list. This is a record that no other celebrity has achieved so far. Today, at 55, she is still one of the most beautiful women, and she attributes her unchanging beauty to several tricks that are worth imitating.

1. Never forgets to wear sunscreen

Julia loves sunbathing and going to the beach with her children, as you might have already seen on her Instagram account. In 2017, she told a magazine: “As a responsible parent with three kids who love to be outdoors, I rely totally on a good sunblock.”

She revealed that she chooses SPF 30 for more active days as it “keeps working no matter how sweaty or how much time we spend in the water! It’s magical.”

2. Never forgets to moisturize her hands

Many people tend to focus only on the face when it comes to skincare, but hands can also show signs of aging. Julia believes that finding a good moisturizer for them is key.

She said, “I put it on my hands after I wash the dishes, and wind up putting it on my elbows and feet. Before you know it, I’ve squeezed this poor little green tube into a twist.”

3. Always washes her face before going to bed

In another of the many interviews she has given, she confessed that an important part of her daily beauty routine consists of applying a really effective eye makeup remover lotion.

“A lot of times you take off your eye makeup at night, and the next morning you wake up and look like you already put makeup on! But this formula gets rid of every stitch of makeup. For more than six years, it’s the one product I’ve used every day. It’s wonderful!”

4. Uses a moisturizing and shiny foundation

Andy Kropa/Invision/East News

Julia Roberts is a busy mom of three, but she always looks radiant and fresh. Genevieve Herr, one of the stars’ makeup artists, revealed that part of the secret to the actress’s natural look is applying a moisturizing, glowing foundation from Lancôme that not only boosts radiance but also reduces fine lines.

This formula lets the skin breathe, and it also has nourishing ingredients such as hyaluronic acid, which works as a plumper, and mandelic acid, which smoothes and refines the skin.

5. Has a simple routine

Stuart Hardy/ABACAPRESS.COM/Abaca/East News

When the actress was asked if her beauty routine was low or high maintenance, she replied, “Low. I could definitely go a little higher.”

Then added: “On a regular day, I’ll just brush my teeth, wash my face, and apply sunblock. I’m a busy mom, and sometimes I just sunblock my kids and run outside and get my nose burned. So I’ve made a stronger effort to take care of myself.”

6. Eats mindfully

Julia Roberts proves that there is no beauty routine that is not complemented by a proper diet. In a newspaper interview, she said, “We definitely try to eat mindfully. As I always say to my kids, ’You have to eat the good stuff to get the good stuff.’”

However, respecting the diet can be very difficult, so her best advice is: “Remain calm, drink your water, get your sleep, and be joyful.”

7. Brushes her teeth with baking soda

PHOTOlink/Everett Collection/East News, Collin Xavier/Image Press Agency ABACA/Abaca/East News

Julia’s smile is her trademark, and fans have always wondered what her secret is to having such perfect teeth. She confessed that she follows the example set by her grandfather.

“I brush [my teeth] with baking soda. [My grandfather] would put a big heaping mound of it on his toothbrush. He had only one cavity in his entire life.”

8. Works out for one hour

Julia Roberts has had to adapt her routine, so she doesn’t need to go to a gym or leave her house, since she is a full-time mom. Because of that, she works out in her home while taking care of her little ones.

She splits her time doing 20 to 40 minutes of step aerobics followed by 40 minutes of bodywork (a series of exercises focused on strengthening the core and reducing inflammation), push-ups, planks (60 seconds each), squats, and crunches. When she can, she alternates this with swimming and running.

9. Isn’t concerned about her age

EAST NEWS, Chris Pizzello/Invision/East News

Julia once said that one of her biggest secrets to staying young is to “try to have some peace with the aging process.”

She has even stepped into controversy by denouncing the pressures on women in Hollywood, as she claimed that she may have risked her career by choosing not to undergo a face-lift.

10. Her biggest secret: being happy

In an interview, Julia said that one of her secrets to stay beautiful and radiant is to be happy. “I think optimism and having a sense of humor definitely help, but happiness is something you cultivate. Once you find it, that’s the key to looking beautiful.”

Coming home from work and being with her husband and children keeps her happy. That’s something that shows in her appearance and is contagious.

Bonus: Julia Roberts’ secret to a happy marriage

In a 2022 interview, the actress revealed the reason why she has remained married to her husband, Danny Moder, for 20 years: “I always say that same thing, and I’m sticking with it,” she said, and then added, “It’s making out. Lots of making out.”
