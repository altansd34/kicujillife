Publish date: 2024-08-12

La Liga features some of the world's highest-paid players. Like players in other top European leagues, La Liga pays clubs millions of Euros. As a result, players who feature in this league usually get handsome pay-checks. Discover the current highest-paid players in La Liga below.

La Liga is one of the wealthiest sports leagues around the globe. The winning club gets around €158 million in prize money. Barcelona and Real Madrid are some of the best La Liga teams to play for because they have the highest average annual pay per player.
