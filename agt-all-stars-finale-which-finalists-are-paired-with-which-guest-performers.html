America’s Got Talent: All-Stars is getting ready to air the finale episode on Monday night. The episode will feature performances from several guests paired with finalists from the show. Ahead of the show, AGT has revealed which finalists will perform with which guests.

AGT: All-Stars Reveals Finale Performance Pairings

During Monday’s finale, American Idol runner-up Adam Lambert will take the stage with aerialist Aidan Bryant and acrobatic trio the Bello Sisters. Former AGT winner Mat Franco will also appear in a performance with young BGT magician Aidan McCann.

We’ll also get to see singer Babyface join forces with saxophonist Avery Dixon, while the band Weezer will perform with Detroit Youth Choir. AGT alum Lindsey Stirling will also be on hand to perform with Filipino couple Power Duo.

Tom Ball will also be performing with AGT alums Voices of Hope Children’s Choir. Their performance of “Who Wants to Live Forever” by Queen was shared as an early release. It also appears that finalists Kodi Lee and Light Balance Kids will be performing together for the finale.

Terry Fator Will Perform with Ana-Maria Mărgean

Fans also correctly predicted that AGT winner Terry Fator would be returning to the All-Stars stage to perform with young ventriloquist Ana-Maria Mărgean. Fator had teased a finale appearance on social media, which clued people in that Ana-Maria would make it to the Finals.

Based on photos released by NBC, it looks like comedian Mike E. Winfield might be the only finalist who isn’t paired up with another performer for the finale. A photo wasn’t released of him performing during the new episode, so we’ll have to wait and see what happens.

During Monday’s two-hour finale, we’ll also find out which of the 11 finalists was voted the winner by the show’s Superfans. Who are you rooting for? Let us know in the comments below, and be sure to watch at 8/7c on NBC.
