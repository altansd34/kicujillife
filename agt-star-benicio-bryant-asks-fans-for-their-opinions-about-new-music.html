America’s Got Talent Season 14 finalist Benicio Bryant recently took to Instagram to share snippets of new music with fans, asking their opinions about which song he should release. The 18-year-old singer released his most recent single, “I Can’t Dance to This,” last summer.

Benicio Bryant Teases Music with Fans on Instagram

All three of the song snippets Bryant posted on Instagram have a great dance sound to them. He asked fans in the post’s caption, “which one is more ‘puty outy’??????” This seems to suggest that he has plans to release at least one of these tracks soon.

“You could have my body, you could take my choice / But baby, you could never, ever take my voice,” Bryant sings on the second track. On the third track, he sings, “You should never, ever dance after dark.”

Fans were divided about which song they liked the most. Some were sure they wanted to hear more of the third song, while others preferred the first two. Several people commented that they would love to hear all three of them released.

The singer even posted an Instagram story asking which demo was fans’ favorite. However, because it was set up as a quiz rather than a poll, we can’t see what the most popular choice was.

Bryant Recently Shared Another Demo

In December, Bryant shared a demo on YouTube for a song called “Melancholy.” The track has a slower tempo than the snippets he posted on Instagram this week. Fans in the comments shared their love for the song.

Last year, Bryant released a new single called “I Can’t Dance to This,” along with a stylish music video featuring a series of glamorous outfits. The singer has been having fun playing around with his look since he appeared on AGT in 2019.

Bryant teased on Instagram in December that exciting things are on the way this year. He wrote, “ok but guys….next year……so many surprises…..” Hopefully that means fans won’t have to wait long to hear some new music releases.
