Camila Cabello Has Reportedly Split From Austin Kevitch Due to &quot;Very Busy&quot; Careers

After developing their romance less than a year ago, former The Voice coach Camila Cabello and Lox Club co-founder Austin Kevitch have reportedly broken up. A newsletter sent to subscribers of the Lox Club dating app revealed that Kevitch is “single again.”

Camila Cabello, Austin Kevitch Have Reportedly Broken Up

Cabello and Kevitch have gone their separate ways after a short-lived relationship. The former Fifth Harmony singer and the CEO of the Lox Club had been linked together since last summer, but now it appears that the couple has officially broken up.

A newsletter sent to subscribers of the dating app announced the news that Kevitch is single once again. The former couple has yet to address the news at the moment but a source close to them has confirmed the public about their breakup.

“Camila and Austin broke up, there’s no bad blood between them and they’re both just very busy in their careers,” The source said.

It’s never easy when two people decide to go their separate ways and these two were no different. Fans of both Cabello and Kevitch were shocked to learn that they had split. Despite the breakup, it appears that both Cabello and Kevitch are on good terms.

They Allegedly Dated For Eight Months

Cabello and Kevitch first sparked romance rumors in June of 2022, after they were spotted spending time together in Los Angeles. After two months, the pair were spotted walking hand in hand together after grabbing some drinks at a local cafe. In November of last year, they were spotted having dinner together in Santa Monica.

We’re not sure how this couple came to be, since Cabello is pretty open about disliking dating apps. On the other hand, Kevitch is very pro-dating apps. His app, The Lox Club, has a vast pool of single women, so it’s possible that he looks for love on there. In October 2022, Cabello told Drew Barrymore that she deleted an app after only 24 hours.

“I was on a dating app for like 24 hours, then I left,” Cabello said. “The first guy that DM’d me was an aspiring singer-songwriter from Nashville. I was just like, I feel weird because somebody could be using me, I mean you don’t know their intentions.”

Cabello was formerly in a relationship with fellow singer Shawn Mendes. After two and a half years together, they announced their separation in November 2021.
