Simon Cowell and his fiancée Lauren Silverman recently opened up about how welcoming their son Eric changed their lives. Cowell said he didn’t expect to be a dad, but since having a child he has “looked at life in a completely different way.”

Simon Cowell, Lauren Silverman Talk About Son Eric

Cowell and Silverman recently spoke to HELLO! in a joint interview after a charity event for the London Music Fund. They spoke about their son Eric, who turned 10 earlier this year. Cowell said that 20 years ago, he “didn’t think I was going to have kids because I thought I was too old.”

“So when I got the news I was going to be a dad – and the first time I saw his scan – I was literally obsessed. Something changed and I looked at life in a completely different way,” Cowell explained. “Every decision I make now I am thinking about Eric. Is it the right decision for him?”

Silverman called Cowell a “workaholic” who used to stay up “until crazy hours,” adding that he “sometimes needs me and Eric to push him to do more normal things and get up at a reasonable hour.” She said Cowell has “definitely got a lot better since we first met.”

Silverman Says Cowell Had ‘Something Missing’ Before Fatherhood

“I think before he had Eric his life was great, and he had so much success,” Silverman said of Cowell’s lifestyle before parenthood. “But there was definitely a void, something missing. And maybe he didn’t even know what that thing was.”

She shared her belief that starting a family made Cowell realize “that actually there’s so much more to life and what’s the point of having all this if you don’t have somebody you want to share it with?” 

Cowell and Silverman got engaged over the Christmas holiday in 2021. Since then, Cowell has talked about planning the wedding, but the couple has yet to walk down the aisle. The Got Talent judge has never been married.
