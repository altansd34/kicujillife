Charli D'Amelio's Wows in Kids' Choice Awards Performance

Publish date: 2024-08-24

The 2023 Kids’ Choice Awards saw a spectacular performance by TikTok star Charli D’Amelio, who co-hosted the show along with Nate Burleson. After winning Season 31 of Dancing with the Stars, D’Amelio was tapped for the hosting role and was thrilled to accept.

Charli D’Amelio Performed The 2023 Kids’ Choice Awards

At just 18 years old, D’Amelio has quickly become a household name thanks to her viral TikTok videos. With her success on DWTS and now the Kids’ Choice Awards, it’s clear that D’Amelio is a rising star with a bright future ahead of her.

During last weekend’s award ceremony, D’Amelio displayed her impressive dance skills, honed on DWTS. Nickelodeon filled the comments section with praise after posting a clip from D’Amelio’s dance on Instagram.

“I’ve been a huge fan of the Kids’ Choice Awards for years, and it’s a dream come true to be co-hosting this year’s show,” D’Amelio said.

Clad in a sparkling crop top, white baggy pants, and white sneakers, the young star wowed the audience with a high-energy dance performance set to Lady Gaga’s “Bloody Mary.” The crowd cheered as D’Amelio, who was also accompanied by backup dancers, executed a flawless routine. Her performance then quickly became the talk of social media, with fans praising her talent and charisma.

Charli D’Amelio Celebrates TikTok Milestone with Family

TikTok superstar D’Amelio recently hit a major milestone, reaching 150 million followers on the platform. To celebrate the occasion, D’Amelio’s family then threw her a surprise party, complete with pink balloons, a custom sign, and a cake that read “Congrats on 150M.”

D’Amelio, known for her dance videos and also fun-loving personality, posted a celebration video on TikTok to share it with her fans. She posted a video on March 1, in which she then walks into a room filled with pink decorations and her family members.

The video quickly went viral, racking up millions of views and also likes within hours of its posting. Fans flooded the comments section with congratulatory messages and also expressions of admiration for D’Amelio and her family.

D’Amelio, who rose to fame on TikTok with her catchy dance videos, has also become one of the most popular social media influencers in the world. She’s currently the second most followed content creator on the platform, and fans wait in anticipation for her to take first place. Senegalese-born Italian social media personality Khabane “Khaby” Lame, who currently has over 155 million followers, holds the current first place.
