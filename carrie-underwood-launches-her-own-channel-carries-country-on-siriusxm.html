Carrie Underwood Launches Her Own Channel 'Carrie's Country' on SiriusXM

Publish date: 2024-08-06

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Getty Images for CMT

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Country music fans are in for a treat as superstar Carrie Underwood announces the exciting news of her collaboration with SiriusXM. The satellite radio service will soon feature an exclusive year-round channel called Carrie’s Country, which is set to debut in June.

Carrie Underwood Gets Channel on SiriusXM

The Grammy-winning singer-songwriter cannot wait to welcome listeners into her world, sharing her love for music that ranges from classic rock to the latest in the country. With an array of original programming lined up, the channel promises to offer unique content for fans and music enthusiasts alike.

The programming on Carrie’s Country will feature an assortment of monthly themed shows, providing an intimate glimpse into Underwood’s life and interests. One such program, “Find Your Path,” will offer valuable advice, and fitness tips from Underwood and her trainer, promoting a healthier lifestyle. Additionally, “Savior Sunday” will showcase Underwood’s gospel catalogue, along with a selection of country tracks close to her heart.

Another exciting segment, titled “Take the Wheel,” will invite guest DJs to share their personal musical preferences, offering listeners a diverse range of songs handpicked by influential figures. Furthermore, the channel will revisit Underwood’s remarkable career, presenting a “soundtrack to her life” that highlights the milestones and musical influences that shaped her journey.

With her Las Vegas residency set to start in June, coinciding with the launch of her new SiriusXM channel, Underwood invites fans and music lovers to embark on a musical journey with her through Carrie’s Country.

Carrie Underwood Visits SiriusXM Miami Studios In Celebration of Carrie’s Country

Fresh off the exciting announcement of her exclusive SiriusXM channel, Carrie’s Country, Underwood paid a visit to the new SiriusXM Miami Studios. Underwood took the opportunity to celebrate the upcoming launch of her 24/7 channel and engage with the SiriusXM team.

During her visit to SiriusXM, Underwood had the privilege of sitting down with the legendary Howard Stern for an in-depth interview and a captivating three-song performance on SiriusXM’s The Howard Stern Show. The conversation with Stern allowed for a deeper exploration of Underwood’s life, career, and the inspirations that have shaped her journey.

As the launch of Carrie’s Country draws near, anticipation builds among country music enthusiasts and loyal fans. With an incredible lineup of artists ranging from Keith Urban and Dolly Parton to Brad Paisley and Miranda Lambert, as well as iconic rock acts like AC/DC, Guns N’ Roses, and The Rolling Stones, the channel promises to be a go-to destination for a diverse and captivating music listening experience.
