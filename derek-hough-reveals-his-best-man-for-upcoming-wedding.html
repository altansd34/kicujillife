Derek Hough Reveals His Best Man For Upcoming Wedding

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Wedding bells are ringing for Derek Hough and his fiancée Hayley Erbert as they dive headfirst into the exciting world of wedding planning. The couple, who have been together for nearly six years, are eagerly anticipating their upcoming nuptials and can’t wait to take that momentous walk down the aisle. While the wedding details are coming together, Hough shared that his best man is a close friend and a fellow Dancing With the Stars professional dancer.

Derek Hough Reveals His Best Man

In an recent interview, the DWTS judge shared some details about their wedding preparations. Hough revealed that choosing the perfect venue was one of their initial decisions, and that his fellow DWTS alum Ballas is the chosen guy to be the best man.

Hough praised Ballas, describing him as “beautiful with words,” and expressing confidence in the heartfelt speech he will deliver on the big day. The friendship between Hough and Ballas goes way back, starting with their ballroom training as teenagers in London. They even formed a band with Hough’s sister Julianne before joining the cast of DWTS.

Although Hough exudes excitement about the wedding, he confessed to feeling a slight nervousness about his vows. However, he quickly reassured that he ultimately feels good about it all. His main focus is to ensure that everything feels effortless and that both he and Erbert are healthy and present throughout the festivities.
