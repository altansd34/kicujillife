Top 7 Best Auditions Ever On 'The Voice UK'

Publish date: 2024-08-16

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing The Voice The Voice UK

Reading Time: 3 minutes

The Voice UK has been a staple of British television for over a decade, and over the years, the show has featured some truly amazing singers. Relive the magical moments that defined the essence of The Voice UK, where dreams were realized, and stars were born, these are the best auditions ever in the history of the show.

Top 7 Best Auditions Ever on The Voice UK

7. Donel Mangena

One of the most memorable Blind Auditions from The Voice UK was Series 7 runner-up Donel Mangena. Mangena, who hails from Zimbabwe, performed “Cold Water” by Major Lazer, Justin Bieber & MØ and it earned him a four-chair turn and a standing ovation. Standing behind stage is his grandmother, who is perhaps even more excited than him.

6. Kevin Simm

Kevin Simm’s audition in Series Five took everyone by surprise. As a former member of the band Liberty X, he had primarily performed as part of a group and never showcased his solo singing abilities. However, when he took the stage singing Sia’s “Chandelier,” it was evident that he had found his true calling.

The power and emotion in his performance were undeniable, and it earned him a four-chair turn from all four coaches. With his exceptional talent, Simm emerged victorious in the series, solidifying his status as a remarkable solo artist.

5. Molly Hocking

In 2019, Molly Hocking took part in the eighth series of The Voice UK, showcasing her incredible talent. The 16-year-old singer from Wales delivered a soul-stirring rendition of “I’ll Never Love Again” by Lady Gaga and Bradley Cooper, leaving the judges in awe of her vocal abilities. As the competition progressed, Hocking’s exceptional talent continued to shine, earning her the title of the series’ winner.

4. Jermain Jackman

Jackman’s audition in Series Three of The Voice UK made waves and remains one of the most memorable moments in the show’s history. Jackman delivered a powerful rendition of “And I Am Telling You I’m Not Going,” which resonated with audiences. In April 2014, he emerged as the winner, becoming the first male to achieve this in the competition.

3. Stevie McCrorie

In 2015, something extraordinary happened on The Voice UK. A young and talented artist named Stevie McCrorie took the stage and performed Kodaline’s “All I Want” with such soul-stirring passion that all four coaches couldn’t help but turn their chairs in awe. With his unwavering determination, McCrorie soared through the rounds, earning the nickname “The Hero.” Finally, on that unforgettable night, as the winner’s name was announced, the crowd erupted in joy, as McCrorie emerged victorious in the competition.

2. Anthonia Edwards

Anthonia Edwards, a remarkable English singer, and nurse hailing from Blackheath, London, etched her name in The Voice UK history. In 2022, her powerful voice resonated throughout the eleventh series as she took the stage to audition. With an awe-inspiring rendition of Katy Perry’s “Unconditionally,” Edwards left the audience and all four coaches spellbound, resulting in a four-chair turn.

Week after week, she continued to captivate viewers with her stunning performances. As the series reached its climactic finale, the announcement reverberated through the air, declaring Edwards as the ultimate champion.

1. Mo Adeniran

In a dazzling display of talent, Mo Adeniran took The Voice UK stage by storm, leaving everyone in awe with his Blind Audition singing Paolo Nutini’s “Iron Sky.” His mesmerizing performance garnered not just one or two, but all four coaches turning their chairs. Ultimately, his exceptional talent and unwavering dedication led him to triumph, emerging as the well-deserved winner of Season Six.
