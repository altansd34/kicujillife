The Masked Singer alum Bella Thorne recently opened up about her decision not to sign sexy photos of herself. The actress said she told off an autograph seeker who presented her with risqué photos of herself, including some from when she was underage.

Bella Thorne Won’t Sign Sexy Photos of Herself

In a recent interview with the Hollywood Reporter, Thorne said she told off a man who wanted her to sign sexy photos of herself, including one from a GQ magazine photoshoot she did. The man was persistent, even after she said she didn’t want to sign the photos.

“I didn’t like it. It was inappropriate,” Thorne said. “There was some stuff there from a Candies campaign that I did when I was 16 and it was clear that he wanted me to sign things that were viewed as sexy and even underage and I was like, ‘Give me something else.’ He said, ‘Aren’t they all sexy?’ No, enough is enough.”

Thorne added that she understands that autograph seekers “have to make money and everyone has a job,” but she shared, “It was just that I was under 18 and it’s inappropriate.”

Thorne Says She Wouldn’t Pose For Those Photos Now

When asked if she ever rethinks some of the photos she took in the past, Thorne responded, “Yeah, definitely, but it’s just that, I wouldn’t do that now. You also can’t spend your time trying to take back these moments or moments that you were too sexy or think that being a woman is only this or that.”

Thorne added that she’s working on a short film “about growing up and being told from a very young age or by experiences that life as a woman means that people are either going to take something from you or you’re going to give it to them.”

Earlier this year, Thorne spoke out about her experiences as a child actor, saying a director accused her of flirting with him when she was 10 years old. She also shared that she was almost fired by Disney at age 14 for wearing a bikini at the beach.
