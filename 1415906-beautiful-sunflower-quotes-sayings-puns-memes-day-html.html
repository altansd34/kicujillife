Beautiful sunflower quotes, sayings, puns, and memes to make your day

Publish date: 2024-08-28

A sunflower is a herbaceous annual plant with a thick hairy erect stem that gives rise to a flower head. Some of them are grown for oil, seeds, and food. The large size of these flowers paired with their beautiful yellow petals makes them exquisite to look at. They are not only beautiful but can teach us important lessons about life. Have a look at some beautiful and inspirational sunflower quotes and images.

From short and sweet to inspirational and meaningful, these sunflower sayings will brighten even the dreariest day.

Beautiful sunflower quotes and sayings

Have a look at some of the beautiful quotes about sunflowers.

  • And here, the sunflower of the spring burns bright in the morning’s beam. - Ebenezer Elliott
  • Keep your face to the sunshine, and you cannot see the shadow. It is what sunflowers do. - Helen Keller
  • True friends are like bright sunflowers that never fade away, even over distance and time. -Marie Williams Johnstone
  • Be like a flower and turn your face to the sun. - Kahlil Gibran
  • And the yellow sunflower by the brook, in autumn beauty stood. -William Cullen Bryant
  • Light-enchanted sunflower, thou who gazest ever true and tender on the sun’s revolving splendour. - Pedro Calderón de la Barca
  • We are all golden sunflowers inside. - Allen Ginsberg
  • Open the bloom of your heart and become a gift of beauty to the world. - Bryant McGill
  • Fame is the scentless sunflower, with a gaudy crown of gold; But friendship is the breathing rose, with sweets in every fold. - Oliver Wendell Holmes
  • Every flower blooms at a different pace. - Suzy Kassem
  • To plant a garden is to believe in tomorrow. - Audrey Hepburn
  • Never look directly at the sun. Instead, look at the sunflower. - Vera Nazarian
