Derek Hough's 'Symphony of Dance' Tour Announces Significant Changes

Derek Hough has sent shockwaves through his fanbase with unexpected changes to the highly anticipated Symphony of Dance tour. Originally scheduled to kick off in September 2023, the tour’s dates have been shuffled around. Due to the nature of the changes, fans have been speculating the reason.

Derek Hough’s Symphony of Dance Tour Changes Dates

This national tour, featuring the Emmy Award-winning choreographer and dancer Hough, promised to be a spectacular showcase of dance and music. With high-energy choreography, stunning stage production, and an array of dance styles, it was going to be a must-see event.

The initial tour schedule, as revealed in the original poster, listed a series of dates starting from September 28, 2023, in Las Vegas, NV, and concluding on December 23, 2023, in Idaho Falls, ID. Fans were gearing up for a journey through various cities, eagerly marking their calendars with these dates.

However, in a recent Instagram story post, Hough shared the original tour poster with a significant update, some of the dates are now different. If you have tickets to this event, be sure to confirm the show’s date ahead of the event.

The caption read, “We’ve made a few changes!” This sudden alteration took fans by surprise and led to intense speculation about the reasons behind it.

Fans ignited discussions on Reddit. User CoffeeAndCurls76 started a thread speculating that the changes might be related to the scheduling of Dancing With The Stars Season 32, which could be moving to Tuesdays. This speculation was rooted in the observation that many of the tour dates that were previously on Tuesdays had been shifted to Mondays.

Derek Hough Shares Photos of His Honeymoon With Hayley Erbert

In addition to his tour date changes, the celebrated dancer and choreographer has also given fans a glimpse into a different kind of adventure, his romantic honeymoon with Hayley Erbert. The couple, who tied the knot on August 29 in Monterey County, California, is now having the time of their lives in the picturesque hills of Tuscany, Italy.

On Tuesday, Hough, 38, took to Instagram to then share a video montage of their honeymoon escapades. The video captures the essence of their love-filled journey, showing the couple’s exploration of Tuscany’s scenic countryside, visits to vineyards, culinary delights, and, of course, moments of dancing and kissing in the idyllic Italian landscape.

This honeymoon getaway comes after the couple’s enchanting wedding ceremony, where they exchanged vows in the presence of 106 guests, officiated by Hough’s brother-in-law. The newlyweds embarked on their Italian adventure, also exploring a range of breathtaking locations, including Lake Como, Positano, the Amalfi Coast, Rome, Tuscany, Florence, and more.
