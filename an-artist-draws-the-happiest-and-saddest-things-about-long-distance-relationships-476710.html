AnArtist Draws the Happiest and Saddest Things About Long-Distance Relationships

Publish date: 2024-08-28

The talented and ironic artist, Tabby (of tabrakadabra) knows about the many discussions and arguments that often come along with long-distance relationships. But despite how skeptical everyone seems to be about dating long-distance, Tabby and her boyfriend continue to keep their relationship going strong. You can read all about the couple’s story by going through these comic illustrations in the article below.

Bright Side has decided to share these illustrations of tenderness, sadness, and unbelievable happiness with all of you. We want to remind everyone who is in a similar situation that your feelings are valid and that it’s worth trying to meet each other halfway while listening only to your heart, and not what other people are saying.
